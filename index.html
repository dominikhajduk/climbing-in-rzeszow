Climbing in Rzeszów

Where can you climb in Rzeszów?

Idea for this page

Here I want collect information in English about places where you can climb in Rzeszów (Poland).

Indoor climbing

In Rzeszów there are three public open indoor climbing walls:

  • Climbing Wall near Technical University
  • Baltoro Climbing Centre
  • Climbing wall near Rzeszów University

The climbing wall near Technical University (Politechnika Rzeszowska)

Climbing wall has webpage also main info is presented in English. The climbing wall you can find on Google Maps. Wall is open from October to June. During summer Holidays wall is generally closed. Exact days and hours are marked on the schedule. If the schedule is empty, wall is probably closed. Wall offers mostly top rope and lead climbing with rope but you can find also some place for bouldering.

Baltoro Climbing Centre

Baltoro climbing centre is the biggest climbing wall in Rzeszów. Here you can find wall webpage. Wall you can find on Google Maps. Unfortunately, there is no English version and webpage is not always up to date so you should look for more details on Facebook. Wall offers nice bouldering possibilities, rope climbing and a lot of training accessories like Bachar ladder or campus, system wall.

Climbing wall near Rzeszów University

The climbing wall has no webpage. For more info you should visit Facebook. It's hard to say when the wall is open. The schedule is not published and you should probably ask using Facebook. Wall is generally vertical (no overhang) and offers rope climbing. Climbing wall on Google Maps.
