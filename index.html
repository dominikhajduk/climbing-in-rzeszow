Idea for this page

Here I want collect information about places where you can climb in Rzeszów (Poland).

Indoor climbing

In Rzeszów there are three main climbing walls:

  • Climbing Wall near Technical University
  • Baltoro climbing centre
  • Climbing wall near Rzeszów University

Climbing wall near Technical University (Politechnika Rzeszowska)

Climbing wall has webpage also main info are presented in English. Climbing wall you can find on Google Maps. Wall is open from October to June. During Holidays wall is generally closed. Exact days and hours are marked in the schedule. If schedule is empty, wall is probably closed. Wall offers mostly top rope and lead climbing with rope but you can find some place for bouldering.

Baltoro climbing centre

Baltoro climbing centre is the biggest climbing wall in Rzeszów. Here you can find wall webpage. Wall you can find on Google Maps. Unfortunately there is no English version and webpage is not always up to date so you should look for more details on Facebook. Wall offer nice bouldering possibilities, rope climbing and a lot of training accessories like Bachar ladder or campus.

Climbing wall near Rzeszów University

Climbing wall has no webpage for more info you should visit Facebook. It's hard to say when wall is open. Schedule is not published and you should probably ask using Facebook. Wall is generally vertical (no overhang) and offers rope climbing. Climbing wall on Google Maps.
